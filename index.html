To use:

  • 1. Click on the name of a Hero with your mouse
  • 2. While holding click down, drag the Hero around
  • 3. Release the mouse with the Hero dragged over a spot in the ranked list to place it there.
  • 4. Releasing the mouse while the Hero is NOT over a list spot will return it to the unranked pool
  • 5. Placing a Hero in a list spot already occupied by another Hero will result in the other Hero being bumped up, down or off the list
